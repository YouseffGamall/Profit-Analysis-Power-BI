# # Product Analysis Dashboard – Power BI Project

## Overview

This project presents a **Product Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, product categories, and sub-categories.
The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify profitable product segments, and detect low-performing areas for better decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Compare product categories based on revenue and profitability.
* Identify the most profitable sub-categories.
* Track product quantity and product count.
* Provide interactive and visual business insights using Power BI.

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

The dashboard includes summary cards for:

* **Total Sales:** 2.30M
* **Total Profit:** 286K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K
* **Total Products:** 1862

These KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

---

### Sales by Category

A column chart displays total sales across major categories:

* Technology
* Furniture
* Office Supplies

This visualization helps compare revenue generation between categories.

---

### Profit by Category

A donut chart represents profit contribution percentages by category:

* Technology
* Office Supplies
* Furniture

This helps identify which category contributes the highest profit.

---

### Profit by Sub-Category

A horizontal bar chart analyzes profit across sub-categories such as:

* Copiers
* Phones
* Accessories
* Chairs
* Tables
* Bookcases

The chart highlights:

* High-performing sub-categories
* Loss-making products
* Profit distribution patterns

---

## Tools & Technologies

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* Copiers and Phones are among the most profitable sub-categories.
* Tables and Bookcases showed negative profit, indicating potential business issues.
* Office Supplies maintained stable profitability performance.
